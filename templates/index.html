About

Tech companies use facial recognition technology (FRT) to learn more about their customers. They use this information to entice, manipulate, and deceive us. Modern FRT is based off Deep Learning, which is a type of artificial neural network system that has revolutionized AI. A deep learning model is a system that predicts some category given an input. In this case, a model can predict a categroy like gender given a picture of your face. This app uses multiple models to predict age, ethnicity, gender, and emotion. Google, Amazon, and Facebook don't need your consent or your profile information to learn lots about you: all they need is a picture of your face.
